Abstract#==#We present a first web-based Visual Analytics framework for the analysis of
multiparty discourse data using verbatim text transcripts. Our framework
supports a broad range of server-based processing steps, ranging from data
mining and statistical analysis to deep linguistic parsing of English and
German. On the client-side, browser-based Visual Analytics components enable
multiple perspectives on the analyzed data. These interactive visualizations
allow exploratory content
analysis, argumentation pattern review, and speaker interaction modeling.
