Abstract#==#Recent advances in distributional semantics combined with the availability of
large-scale diachronic corpora offer new research avenues for the Digital
Humanities. JeSemE, the Jena Semantic Explorer, renders assistance to a
non-technical audience to investigate diachronic semantic topics. JeSemE runs
as a website with query options and interactive visualizations of results, as
well as a REST API for access to the underlying diachronic data sets.
